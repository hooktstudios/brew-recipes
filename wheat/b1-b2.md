# B1-B2

> A real good classic hefeweizen

## Brewing notes

### 2014-09-06

## Mash

```mash
3.75 kg wheat malt
2.5 kg two-row malt
29l starting water
```

- 60 minutes mash @ ~68 (69 to 67) celsius (got 71 celsius before drop)
- Mash pH : ?

### 2013-06-23

## Mash

```mash
3 kg wheat malt
2 kg two-row malt
26l starting water
2l toping
```

- 60 minutes mash @ ~68 (69 to 67) celsius (got 72 celsius before drop)
- Mash pH : 5.4

## Boiling & hops

- 60 minutes boiling
- 60 min. 27g Saaz

## Yeast

3068 Weihenstephan Weizen (Wyeast Lab)
